Our in vitro testing system uses human cell models and enables artificial intelligence-based chemical screening with in-house developed software. Current procedures for regulatory risk assessment of chemicals and drugs regarding their potential to cause developmental neurotoxicity are time-consuming, expensive, and ethically controversial due to the use of laboratory animals.
